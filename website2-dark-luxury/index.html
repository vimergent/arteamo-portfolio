About Studio

Founded in 2008 by Eng. Petya Petrova, Studio Arteamo has established itself as a premier interior design studio, creating spaces that transcend the ordinary.

With over 15 years of excellence, we have transformed countless residential and commercial spaces across Bulgaria. Our philosophy centers on the belief that every space is an opportunity to express ideas and emotions through design.
