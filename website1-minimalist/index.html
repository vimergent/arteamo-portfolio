About Studio Arteamo

Founded in 2008 by Eng. Petya Petrova, Studio Arteamo has become one of the leading names in interior design.

With extensive experience in both public and private projects, our studio has earned recognition through multiple awards including the prestigious "Bathroom of the Year" competition by Ideal Standard.

Awards & Recognition

  • 2009 - First Place, 3D Bathroom Project
  • 2010 - Third Prize for "Peace and Relaxation" Project
  • 2013 - Ideal Standard Bathroom of the Year for "Jazz Standard"

Our Services

Interior Design

We start with you - your desires, lifestyle, and aesthetics. Our team develops personalized concepts that combine avant-garde design with functionality. From initial sketches and 3D visualizations to detailed technical drawings, we create spaces that not only look impressive but feel like home.

Project Supervision

To ensure our vision becomes reality without compromise, we offer author supervision services. We oversee the entire execution process - from material selection to coordination with contractors. Our goal is to ensure flawless quality, deadline compliance, and project adherence down to the last detail.
